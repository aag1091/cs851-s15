About Casio KeyboardsCasio keyboards are produced primarily in Japan by the shriro company. Casio keyboards are not new to the music industry; they have been around since the early 80s when they introduced the CZ101 synthesizer.Casio keyboards are made for novices, professional keyboard players and even for people who like to carry their music with them. Casio keyboards are popular because they are not very expensive and they are loaded with lots of musical features.Used Casio keyboards are quite common and you can get them in most musical instrument stores. Casio keyboards are also sold on auction sites. Casio keyboards are a good choice for a beginner and WK series Casio keyboards are rugged and travel worthy.Because Casio keyboards are geared to beginners, its pianos and keyboards can be used for family gatherings, churches and other uses where simple sounds and realistic pianos sounds are needed.
