Both business start-up entrepreneurs and existing business owners need to protect their assets and family in their personal life and likewise they need to protect their business assets. This workshop will help new and existing business owners understand business insurance and how it will help them manage risk.Business insurance and risk management topics presented at this workshop will include:* What goes into insurance rates.* Real life claims examples.* Top 10 coverage gaps: It is not what is covered - It is what is NOT covered.* Tips on how to save money right now.* It's not all about the cheapest "quote."* Risk management 101.* What to look for in an Agency/Agent - more than just insurance products.* Building a Professional Service Provider team to help you be successful.
