AllAfrica publishes around 2,000 reports a day from more than 130 news organizations and over 200 other institutions and individuals, representing a diversity of positions on every topic. We publish news and views ranging from vigorous opponents of governments to government publications and spokespersons. We operate from Cape Town, Dakar, Lagos, Monrovia, Nairobi and Washington DC.
