Versatile rapper and song writer PEPENAZI who featured in headies Album of the year 2014 “Baddest guy ever Liveth” Motivation and street acclaimed album “STREET OT” in Usain Bolt P has teamed up with rap heavy weight Olamide again as they release the much anticipated single ADAM.The song titled ADAM is a very conscious tune and both artistes did not hesitate to show off their intellectual creativity and lyrical prowess on the track.The song gives a brief overview of the current state of the Nigerian music industry. This tune was produced by OGA JOJO and mixed by prolific mix master, INDOMIX.
