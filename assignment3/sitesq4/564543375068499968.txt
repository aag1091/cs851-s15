How do you go about building an online community that will allow you to regain control over your message and information, while simultaneously engaging your consumers and keep them coming back?This paper details key learnings from experiences designing and implementing online communities. Several rules of thumb are presented for the strategic planning phase; some practical tips on architecting and designing a site that will foster a sense of community among your members; and a number of best practices for organizations looking to enter, interact with, and successfully manage the community once it is running.
