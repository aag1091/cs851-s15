On Sat Aug 31st Nashville's Artist to the Stars, Corey Frizzell, unveiled Loretta Lynn's commissioned portrait on stage during her show at the Loretta Lynn Ranch. Before it was unveiled on stage Loretta got a first look at it during a press conference with The Tennessean, Nashville's Chanel 4 News, Billboard Magazine and Country Weekly Magazine. When asked what she thought of the portrait, Loretta said, "She loved it. It reminded her of her favorite song, "Blue Ky Girl." She then then went out on stage and sang the song.
