Young Arsenal starlet Jon Toral is loving life at Championship side Brentford and believing joining the Bees was a masterstroke in his budding career.The 20-year-old Spanish midfielder joined Arsenal's youth ranks in 2011 from Barcelona, arriving with a big reputation and tipped to succeed.Toral linked up with Brentford on a season-long loan last summer after Gunners boss Arsene Wenger decided first team football would benefit the midfielder and he has been regularly in Mark Warburton's side as they have flown high in the Championship.And Toral feels joining Brentford was a move which is paying off in spades.
