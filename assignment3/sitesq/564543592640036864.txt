The Popsters BiographyThis band from Sicily (Italy) started in March 1999, when the singer and guitar player of the great Italian punkrock band Semprefreski,who both shared a passion for poppy punkrock bands like Descendents and All, teamedup with drummer Claudio Pallino. After playing many covers, soon they started writing their own songs withstrong melodies and love lyrics and around December 1999 they started playing live in their own town Palermo and also around Sicily.
