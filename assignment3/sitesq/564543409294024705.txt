Country A Cappella Group Home Free Debut ‘Everything Will Be Okay’ Music VideoCountry a cappella group Home Free have released a music video for their single ‘Everything Will Be Okay.’ The song follows their widely successful Christmas videos, ‘Angels We Have Heard on High’ and ‘O Holy Night,’ which collectively garnered more than six million views.The five-man group shows off their flawless vocals while singing, “All I want to do is be with you / And all I want you to know is, I ain’t lettin’ go / All I want to feel is that we’ve got something real / And all I want you to say is, ‘Everything will be okay.’”‘Everything Will Be Okay’ is from Home Free’s debut album, ‘Crazy Life,’ which was released on Columbia Records last year, following their win in Season 4 of the a cappella singing competition ‘The Sing-Off’ on NBC. The group sang Florida Georgia Line‘s ‘Cruise’ and ‘Ring of Fire’ by Johnny Cash during their time on the show, before securing their first-place finish with Hunter Hayes‘ ‘Crazy.’Home Free will spend much of 2015 on the road, including two shows in Michigan this month.
