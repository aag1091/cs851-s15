"We Are Infinite" is the debut single from Starling Glow, remixed by Dave Aude (Katy Perry, Lady Gaga, One Direction).Liz Anne Hill took about 15 years and one busted romance to create the songs that make up her band Starling Glow's self-titled debut album, a potpourri of modern pop musical styles that ranges from pop and rock to dance and country.Grammy-nominated producer, songwriter and musician Billy Mohler—one-time member of L.A. band The Calling, who has also played or worked with Smashing Pumpkins, Macy Gray, Liz Phair, Kelly Clarkson, AWOLNation and skateboard pro Tony Hawk—produced their new record, due out in 2014.
