The Mary Sue Is Looking For Science And Tech Contributors!The Mary Sue is looking for contributors, and you could be our next rad writer! Yes, you!Though The Mary Sue’s contributor form is always open, we are specifically looking for writers who can provide us with science and technology coverage. Particularly, we’d like applications from people who are interested in writing stories on women in STEM, be they reports, commentary, or creative pieces. Though your pitches don’t have to be focused on women in STEM, those usually go over well.
