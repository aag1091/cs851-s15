Welcome to Season 8 of The Spoon Ultra Hardcore series.This being the eight season, we will be doing teams of two with 20 people randomly scattered in a 2000 by 2000 border. The goal is to be the last player standing.Ultra Hardcore is a gamemode originally created by Guude with the group of Mindcrack. It's vast amount of popularity comes from involving PVP and survival aspects all in one gamemode. The only ways to recover health in this is with golden apples and golden heads.Golden apples require a 8 gold ingots and an apple to craft.Golden heads require 8 gold ingots and a player head to craft.
