Clean Bandit are collaborating with Microsoft to help create a music video for their next single. For the first time ever, the band are collaborating with YouTube creators, Włodek Markowicz and Karol Paciorek from Poland. Authors of the well-known online entertainment show - Lekko Stronniczny.The #LumiaMusic collaboration will see the journey that Clean Bandit take in making and producing their next music video with the help of Wlodek and Karol and their Microsoft Lumia phones. The three part series will provide exclusive access to the bands idea generation, life on location shooting the music video and the editing and grading process.Subscribe to get all the latest on Clean Bandit’s next music video, starting with Episode 1 which was filmed in London.
