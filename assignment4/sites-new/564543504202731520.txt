EE British Academy Film Awards in 2015 programme – BirdmanEE British Academy Film Awards in 2015 programme – BirdmanThe official programme given to attendees at the EE British Academy Film Awards on 8 February 2015. Five variant covers were created by artist Malika Favre, each one representing a nominee in the Best Film category: Birdman, Boyhood, The Grand Budapest Hotel, The Imitation Game and The Theory of Everything. The brochure includes exclusive interviews with new Fellow Mike Leigh and Christine Langan, head of BBC Films, which was presented with the Outstanding British Contribution to Cinema award. It also includes a special tribute to former BAFTA chairman, trustee and president Lord Attenborough, and a bespoke photography essay focusing on ‘Women In Film’.
