Billy was one of the most prolific tournament winners on the PGA Tour from the mid-1950s to the mid-1970s. After his professional career, Casper was a designer for many golf courses, such as The Highlands, The Palm and Eagle Crest in Sun City Summerlin, Nevada. As of 2011, his management company, Billy Casper Golf, is the second-largest operator of golf courses in the United States, and manages more than 140 golf facilities. Casper died at his home in Springville, Utah on February 7, 2015, aged 83.
