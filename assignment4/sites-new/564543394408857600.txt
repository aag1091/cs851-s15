Friday, December 5, 2014A 5 piece band. Trevor Keyboards/guitar/vocals, Ben Bass guitar/guitar/vocals, Nick lead guitar, Baptiste Drums, Ashley female vocals. “About You” was written by Trevor Dawson and recorded at their own studio, Dusk to Dawn Studios. They have two sound engineers, Benoit (studio engineer) and Gordon (Live engineer). They are all English except Baptiste (French) and Benoit (French but completely bilingual). The studio and the band are based in Northern France in Le Touquet, Nord pa de Calais.
