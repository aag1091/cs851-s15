Horace comes to Oxford’s Jam Factory 29th March – with a real chameleon!We are having an event at The Jam Factory Arts Centre, 27 Park End Street, Oxford OX1 1HU on Sunday 29th March from 10.00am – 12 noon. We will be reading extracts, signing copies of Horace Burp and organising activities for children. Dave Shaw, an inspirational rain forest expert, will also be at the event accompanied by a real chameleon. Plenty of fun so why not come along? The Jam Factory is a good spot for a bite to eat afterwards too…
