Want something sweet that’s not fruit? Treat yourself with a piece of dark chocolate. A little bit of dark chocolate every day has been found to be good for you, as long as the chocolate is at least 65% cocoa and of good quality.Just some of the facts about dark chocolate:It is good for your heart – it increases blood flow to the arteries and heart, reducing risks of blood clots, maybe lowering blood pressureIt is good for your brain – it increases blood flow to the brain increasing cognitive performance, improving mood, focus, and problem solvingIt is nutritious for you – it contains fiber and minerals such as potassium, copper, magnesium and ironIt contains antioxidants – among its benefits, the possible prevention of diseases from cancers to Alzheimer’s
