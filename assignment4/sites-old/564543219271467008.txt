Pidgin English should be introduced in Nigerian schools – TuFace IdibiaAfro pop living legend 2Face Idibia has suggested that Pidgin English should be introduced in Nigerian schools to aid communication and transfer knowledge to kids in rural areas who use Pidgin English as their second language.The father made the statement at the MTV Base #ChooseOrLose Roundtable which will premiere this Friday.“For me, they can gather some linguistic professors and let them package pidgin English properly,” he said.He acknowledged that this may be the most effective way of tackling the education deficit in parts of the country with a language barrier.
