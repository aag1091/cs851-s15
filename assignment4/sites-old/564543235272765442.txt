The Kentucky Wildcats are on the road against the Florida Gators in an attempt to keep their undefeated streak alive. With the Wildcats down two points in the second half, center Willie Cauley-Stein tied the game in dramatic fashion.The Wildcats' Andrew Harrison found Cauley-Stein running the break, and the seven-footer finished with authority over Florida's Devin Robinson.Cauley-Stein drew a foul on the play and hit his free throw to give Kentucky the lead.
