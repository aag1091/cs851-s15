The Grammy’s just announced via Twitter that Tiesto takes home the Grammy award for Best Remix Recording (Non-Classical) for for his Birthday Treatment remix of ‘All Of Me‘. This is the producers first Grammy win and second nomination. He was last nominated for Best Electronic/Dance album in 2008. Follow along with John Legend, the original artist of ‘All of Me’, and Kaskade by tweeting Tiesto a big congratulations on the win.
