John Bonamego, former Detroit Lions ST coach, has been named the head coach of Central Michigan University, replacing Dan Enoa, who departed to take the OC job at Arkansas. This is Bonamego's first head coaching job; his last college experience was as an assistant head coachat Army in 1998. Bonamego has been in the NFL at various levels since then.
