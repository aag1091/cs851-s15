My Life in Ten Seconds: Designer Diane von FurstenbergDiane von Furstenberg hardly needs any introduction. With New York Fashion Week fall/winter 2015 shows rapidly approaching, we got up close and personal with the designer with a quick and intimate survey of questions that delve into her new collection (no spoilers, promise) and her personal life—that takes all of 10 seconds to read.
